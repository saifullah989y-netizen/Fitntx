WHO WE ARE.

FITNTX is a global sports networking agency specializing in consulting and recruitment for students, athletes, professionals, and organizations across all sports and disciplines.

We deliver our services through a network of specialized Representatives — matched to users and organizations by sport, discipline, region, and service line. Whether you're an athlete looking for your next opportunity, a student mapping your career, or an organization seeking talent and growth, we have a Representative who owns your outcome.
